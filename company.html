How Does GNG Work?

GNG exist on blockchain using the ethereum platform as ERC20 token. These transport protocols consist of open source software that interface with blockchains to allow for the issuance and redemption of cryptocurrency tokens, in our case, GNG tokens are 100% backed by British Pound sterling (£) reserves.

GNG tokens are redeemable and exchangeable pursuant to AGLPay Limited's terms of service. The conversion rate is 1 GNG token equals 1 GBP (£).

The GNG Platform is fully reserved when the sum of all tokens in circulation is less than or equal to the value of our reserves. Through etherscan balance tool, anyone can view both of these numbers on a daily basis.
